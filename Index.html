Electropathy courses eligibility

1. BEMS (Bachelor of Electropathy Medicine and Surgery)

Duration: 4.5 years (3.5 years academics + 1 year internship). Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology). Content: Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology, Herbal Medicine, Electro-Herbal Treatment. Scope: Private practice as an Electropathy consultant, herbal product developer.

2. DEMS (Diploma in Electropathy Medicine and Surgery)

Duration: 1-2 years. Eligibility: 10th ya 12th pass. Content: Basic knowledge of herbal and electro-magnetic remedies. Scope: Assistant roles in alternative medicine clinics or self-practice.

3. MD (Doctor of Medicine in Electropathy)

Duration: 2-3 years. Eligibility: BEMS ya equivalent degree. Content: Advanced herbal treatment techniques, research in alternative therapies. Scope: Specialist in Electropathy, research, and teaching.

4. Certificate Courses in Electropathy

Duration: 6 months to 1 year. Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass (varies by institute). Content: Basic herbal and electro-homeopathic remedies. Scope: Basic-level herbal practitioner or product consultant.

5. Electropathy Pharmacy Courses

Duration: 1-2 years (Diploma or Certificate). Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB. Content: Manufacturing, dispensing, and usage of herbal medicines. Scope: Work in herbal pharmaceutical companies or start your own business.
